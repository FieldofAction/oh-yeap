# Design Infrastructure

*A standalone working definition.*

**Design infrastructure is the apparatus that stabilizes an attractor in made things, so the realities worth participating in can emerge.**

It runs on one chain, with the six organs slotted at the artifact layer:

```
source quality → conditions → organs → field → return
```

---

## Source quality (the underneath)

The attractor the whole apparatus serves. For FOA it is **Contact**. This is the deepest layer, the thing the six organs exist to gather and transmit.

## Condition Sets

The minimum conditions under which that quality reliably produces its reality. The translation layer between a quality and a made thing.

## The six organs (the infrastructure itself)

How the attractor gets stabilized and carried into artifacts.

- **Research** reads the ground and finds the conditions worth designing for.
- **Design** gives the conditions form.
- **Thought forms** are the models and patterns that let the form repeat. The shared language layer, where Pattern Language and Relational Design live.
- **Brand** is the coherent signal that makes the whole body read as one.
- **Writing** is the record that makes the work legible and lets it travel without you in the room.
- **Production** is the machine that makes all of it repeatable at volume. Press, Nest, the engineering.

## Field

What the artifacts generate once they are out. Attention, trust, belonging, participation, set by the quality encoded.

## Return

What reality sends back over long horizons. For FOA, **Coherence**. The signal that closes the loop.

---

## The governing discipline: authorship

The organs are attractor amplifiers. They exist to encode the source quality so the return is a coherent field. Authorship keeps the loop closed.

- **Gratitude** reads what already emerged.
- **Condition Sets** shape what emerges next.
- **Leadership** discerns when to observe, when to act, and when to leave reality undistorted.

Empire runs open loop: act, alter conditions, force others to adapt, and refuse the return signal. Authorship runs closed loop: set the conditions, stay in contact with what reality returns, let the return revise the next condition set. Power that needs to feel powerful is power that has stopped reading the boomerang.

---

The six organs are the body. The attractor is what they serve. The return is how it stays honest.
